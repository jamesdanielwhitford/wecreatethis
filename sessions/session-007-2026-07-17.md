# Session 007 - 2026-07-17

## Summary

Site-wide caching architecture rework: every app's service worker moves to a single canonical strategy, network-first with a 3s timeout and cache fallback ("fresh when online, cached when offline"). Kills the version-bump-to-deploy ritual and the three divergent copy-pasted SW generations. Executed with Haiku subagents doing atomic per-app rewrites against a canonical exemplar, orchestrated and verified by the main session.

## Decisions

- **Canonical strategy**: network-first with `fetchWithTimeout` (3000ms, AbortController), cache fallback, offline navigation fallback to the app shell, install-time pre-cache of ASSETS, normalizeUrl kept as a safety net for stray `.html` URLs. Root `sw.js` (v25) is the exemplar all apps follow.
- **Canonical URLs**: extensionless everywhere; internal `.html` links being fixed in git-notes, longform, pokemon, starrynight.
- **Version bumps now near-obsolete**: with network-first, deploys reach users immediately; CACHE_NAME bumps only purge deleted files. All SWs bumped once for this migration.
- **Preserved exceptions**: blog SW untouched (already network-first for content, session-006 design); perfectday tile passthrough + CDN branch; symbolicritual media cache (R2/LRU); tarot CACHE_DECK/REMOVE_DECK message handler; birdle + chessle cross-origin caching (no timeout on cross-origin).
- **Local testing**: `npx wrangler pages dev .` is the standard; `dev/serve.json` cleanUrls hack obsolete (deletion of `dev/` blocked by permissions, left for James).
